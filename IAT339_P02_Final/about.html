Wonder Cake

Wonder cake incorporated on December 13, 2002, is engaged in the bakery business that began with a long time love for art and cakes. Wonder cake is a family owned business passionate for making beautiful and customized cakes. All of cakes are homemade from scratch by a group of whimsical professionals who love turning visions into a reality. Wonder cake shares a passion for real food and are committed to making many more great cakes in a traditional homemade way. Wonder cake is open seven days a week. Wonder cake now offers a daily showcase service, cheesecakes, mousse, and macarons.
